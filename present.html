The classification of Englsih slang

Computer slang can be devided into several groups:

  • Designation of people (guys, gf, mom)
  • Designation of human qualities (mummy's son)
  • Designation of objects, places (Ebooks, ICQ, FAQ)
  • Actions (to troll, I wanna ask u a qstn, CUL8r)
  • Assessment of the situation (ASAP, cool, dunno)
  • Expressing emotions (wow, oof, LOL, OMG)

What and why

The computer slang is an informal layer of language, which consists of words denoting objects of the field of professional activities of computer specialists

Its functions:

  1. Makes people with the same interests closer to each other
  2. The great emotional side
  3. The cultural language code
  4. The economy of time and space

The methods for the formation of computer slang units

  • acronymizing (AFK, ASAP)
  • word composition (nanocomputer)
  • conversion (to program - a program)
  • prefixing (news-group, note-book)
  • charade words (p2p, f2p)
  • consonant slangisms (Internet Exploiter)
  • extreme generalisation (win, disgust)
  • blending (abandonware)
  • affixation (rubber ducking)
